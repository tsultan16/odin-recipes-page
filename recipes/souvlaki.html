Souvlaki

Description

Simple to make, loaded with classic Greek flavours: chicken marinated in lemon, garlic and dried oregano then threaded onto skewers. Served with a homemade Tzatziki, all bundled up in pita bread. Or in my case, a simple homemade flatbread thatâ€™s made without yeast!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb/740 g chicken thigh fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves , minced
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 - 3/4 tsp salt
  • black pepper
  • flatbreads / wraps / pita bread
  • lettuce
  • tomato slices
  • Lebanese cucumbers
  • 1 1/4 cups / 300 g plain Greek yoghurt
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar (or red wine or apple cider vinegar)

Steps

  1. Place chicken and Marinade into a bowl and set aside to marinate for at least 3 hours to overnight.
  2. Thread chicken onto 8 skewers.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat (or BBQ). Cook skewers for 3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  4. Cut the cucumber in half lengthwise. Use a teaspoon to scrape the watery seeds out. Coarsely grate the cucumber using a box grater. Then wrap in paper towels or a tea towel and squeeze to remove excess liquid.
  5. Place cucumber in a bowl. Add remaining ingredients then mix to combine into tzatziki. Set aside for at least 20 minutes for the flavours to meld.
  6. Serve Chicken Souvlaki with tzatziki, flatbreads (make your own!), lettuce and tomato slices.
